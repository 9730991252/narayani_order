Shipping Policy

For International buyers, orders are shipped and delivered through registered international courier companies and/or international speed post only.

Orders are typically shipped within 3–5 days, or as per the delivery date agreed upon during order confirmation. Delivery of the shipment is subject to the norms of the courier company or postal service.

NarayaniOrder.com is not responsible for delays caused by courier companies or postal authorities. We guarantee dispatch of the consignment to the courier/post within 3–5 days from order and payment confirmation, or as per agreed delivery schedule.

Delivery will be made to the address provided by the buyer at the time of order. Confirmation of delivery will be sent to the buyer's registered email ID.
